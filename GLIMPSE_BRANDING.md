# 🎯 GLIMPSE - Official Project Branding & Guidelines

## Project Announcement

**GLIMPSE** is an AR-powered price comparison app for iOS that enables users to instantly see prices from 100+ retailers by simply pointing their camera at any product.

---

## 📌 Project Identity

### Official Name
**GLIMPSE**

### Tagline
"Get a Glimpse of Every Price"

### Domain
glimpse.ar (primary target: Argentina market)

### Vision Statement
*Enable shoppers to make informed purchasing decisions by providing instant, multi-source price comparisons through AR-powered visual recognition.*

---

## 🎨 Branding Elements

### Logo Concept
An eye symbol combined with AR elements, suggesting:
- Vision/viewing (core concept of "glimpse")
- AR technology (camera-based)
- Quick glance (fast, instant)
- Light/shine (etymological origin of "glimpse")

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Electric Blue (#007AFF) - Modern, tech-forward, trust
- **Secondary:** White (#FFFFFF) - Clean, minimal
- **Accent:** Teal/Cyan - Energy, real-time, innovation

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Clean, modern sans-serif (SF Display or similar)
- **Body:** Clear, readable sans-serif (SF Pro or similar)
- **Style:** Minimalist, approachable

### Visual Identity
- Bright, energetic feel (reflects "glimpse" = light/shine)
- AR elements (lens overlays, viewfinder aesthetics)
- Fast, instant (motion lines, quick transitions)

## 🎯 Messaging Framework

### Core Message
GLIMPSE transforms shopping by giving users instant price transparency. No more wondering if you're getting the best deal.

### Key Benefits
1. **Instant** - See prices immediately via camera
2. **Comprehensive** - Compare 100+ retailers at once
3. **Easy** - No typing, no scanning, just point
4. **Trustworthy** - Real-time, multi-source pricing

### Audience Segments
- **Smart Shoppers:** People who value deals and comparison
- **Budget-Conscious:** Those looking to save money
- **Tech-Savvy:** Early adopters of AR technology
- **Time-Pressed:** Users who want quick answers

## 📊 Brand Values

### Innovation
GLIMPSE leverages cutting-edge AR and vision technology to solve real shopping problems.

### Transparency
Instant access to prices from multiple sources - no hidden information.

### Simplicity
Elegant, intuitive design that anyone can use.

### Reliability
Real-time data from trusted retailers, always accurate and up-to-date.

## 📝 Brand Voice

### Tone
- **Friendly:** Approachable, not corporate
- **Direct:** Clear, no jargon
- **Helpful:** Always serves the user's interests
- **Modern:** Tech-forward but accessible
- **Honest:** Transparent about what GLIMPSE does

### Writing Style
- Short, punchy sentences
- Active voice preferred
- User-centric language ("You," "Your")
- Action-oriented verbs
- Avoid marketing clichés

### Example Messaging
✅ "See prices instantly"
✅ "Get a better deal"
✅ "Compare 100+ stores at once"
❌ "Revolutionary price comparison paradigm"
❌ "Disruptive shopping platform"

## 🎓 Brand Guidelines for Agents

### When Working on GLIMPSE

**Use consistent terminology:**
- Product: GLIMPSE (not "the app" or "the platform")
- Feature: Glimpse prices / Get a glimpse
- Action: Point camera → Glimpse

**Maintain brand voice:**
- Keep it simple and direct
- Focus on user benefit
- Use modern, tech-friendly language

**Apply brand values:**
- Innovation → Use latest tech
- Transparency → Show data sources
- Simplicity → Clean, intuitive design
- Reliability → Accurate, real-time data
- Empowerment → Give users control
